# Open Transit Data - Bus
### Delhi Open Bus Transit Data for Travel Time Prediction

## About Dataset
### Published by:
Department of Transport (Govt of NCT of Delhi) in association with IIIT-Delhi, transit datasets (static and dynamic/real-time) on Open Transit Data for enterprises, third-party developers, researchers, and other members of the public to promote collaboration and co-creation of innovative and inclusive transport solutions.

*All credits to the respective owners*

This Dataset contains raw .txt files, with un-processed data.

### Routes
Information on bus routes including route IDs and descriptions.
### Shapes
GPS coordinates of bus routes, useful for calculating distances between stops.
### Stop Times
Scheduled arrival and departure times of buses at stops for specific trips.
### Stops
Information on bus stop locations including GPS coordinates.
### Trips
Information on trips including route IDs and service IDs.
